Hawaii

Hawaii is a U.S. state located in the Pacific Ocean.

Here is some facts about Hawaii:

  • Nickname: "The Aloha State"
  • Statehood: "1959; 50th state"
  • Population (as of March 2021): "1,411,116"
  • Capital: "Honolulu"
  • Biggest City: "Honolulu"
  • Abbreviation: "HI"
  • State bird: "nene, the Hawaiian goose"
  • State flower: "yellow hibiscus"
